About this website



Welcome to Jeny's Beverage Recommendations!

Your friendly companion on a delightful journey of beverage discovery.



With over a decade of experience in the world of hospitality, including her role as a Restaurant Supervisor and Team Lead in a renowned restaurant at Atlantis, The Palm, Dubai, Jeny brings a wealth of knowledge and a warm, inviting spirit to your glass.


Jeny's mission is simple: to be your trusted guide in exploring the world of beverages. Whether you're seeking the perfect cocktail for a celebration or a wine recommendation for a quiet evening, we're here to help. Jeny's expertise and enthusiasm for mixology are at your service.

MOJITO

  • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water (club soda)

DAIQUIRI

Hover card

FROZEN STRAWBERRY DAIQUIRI

  • 2 oz (60 ml) white rum
  • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 oz (22 ml) simple syrup (or more to taste)
  • 1 cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • Ice

CUBA LIBRE

CUBA LIBRE

  • 2 oz (60 ml) white rum
  • 4-6 oz (120-180 ml) cola
  • Fresh lime juice (typically half a lime)
  • Ice
  • Lime wedges for garnish

MARGARITA

Hover card

Frozen Lime Margarita

  • 2 oz (60 ml) tequila
  • 1 oz (30 ml) triple sec (orange liqueur)
  • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz (30 ml) simple syrup
  • Ice
  • Salt (for rimming the glass)
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Video Tutorial

MAI TAI

Hover card

MAI TAI

  • 2 oz (60 ml) light rum
  • 1 oz (30 ml) dark rum
  • 1 oz (30 ml) lime juice
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) orange liqueur (e.g., triple sec)
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) orgeat syrup (almond syrup)
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) simple syrup
  • Ice
  • Pineapple or cherry for garnish

Video Tutorial

MANHATTAN

Hover card

MANHATTAN

  • 2 oz (60 ml) rye whiskey or bourbon
  • 1 oz (30 ml) sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Ice
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish (optional)
  • Orange twist for garnish (optional)
